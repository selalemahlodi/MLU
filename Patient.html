MediLineUp is an Electronic Health Record System (EHRs) and health care management database for patient details/profiles in South Africa. Its aim is to provide the safety of patients' files and medical records. The central focus of our software is to record patients' files via the cloud, especially in the era of the Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR). This approach ensures the safety of data during natural disasters, relocating to new locations, and makes it easier for both administration and doctors to locate and access patient details.
